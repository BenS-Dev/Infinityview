FAQ

South Florida Window Cleaning Questions, Answered

How often should Fort Lauderdale homes schedule window cleaning?

Coastal properties benefit from quarterly exterior cleanings to combat salt air buildup. Interior windows are typically serviced twice per year, or more frequently for showcase views.

What makes pure-water window cleaning better for oceanfront glass?

Deionized water removes all minerals, so windows dry spot-free—even under intense Florida sun. The process is safe for landscaping and won’t etch delicate finishes.

Do you offer maintenance plans for condos and commercial buildings?

Yes. We partner with property managers to create customized schedules for high-rises, luxury condos, hotels, and retail storefronts throughout Broward and Miami-Dade counties.

Are you insured for ladder and aerial lift work?

Absolutely. Infinity View carries full liability and workers’ compensation insurance and follows OSHA best practices for high-access cleaning.
