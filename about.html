About Us

Our Mission

Infinity View was founded by two siblings at Asper School of Business who saw an opportunity to combine eco-friendly deionized water cleaning with old-school customer care. We deliver streak-free windows, gutters, and eavestroughs without harsh chemicals—backed by transparent pricing and a promise to treat your home like our own.

Meet the Founders

David and Daniel Bokser

Co-Founders

Current Asper School of Business students, David and Daniel split their time between classes and running operations at Infinity View. They're grinding hard to secure their dreams and can't wait for what's next. Together, they bring a fresh perspective to the window cleaning industry with their commitment to eco-friendly practices and exceptional customer service.
